Catching
Catching pokemon is the most basic task in PokemonGo, in order to do so a player must have pokeballs. A pokemon is caught when it is hit with a pokeball and shakes 3 times. You can increase chances of capture by great/ultra ball use, raspberries and nice/great/excellent/spin throws. Great/Ultra balls have a chance at dropping at pokestops. Raspberries also drop at pokestops. Lastly throwing the ball is a large part, to get a nice/great/excellent throw you must hit the small ring on the pokemon. To get a spin ball you must rotate the ball before throwing by drawing small circles on the ball, then hit the pokemon.
Teams
Teams in Pokemon go are Instinct, Valor and Mystic. So far there are no identifiable perks to any team. when you reach level 5 you are forces to choose one of the three teams. This lets you participate in gym battles.
Gyms
Gyms are places where teams battle to control a area, opposing teams are allowed to attack gyms while players of the already controlled gym are allowed to train the gym to put more pokemon in to defend it. Holding gyms for 21 hours then gives 10 pokecoins that you claim in the shop. This is the only way to get pokecoins without hacking, or paying.
Eggs
Eggs are received from pokestops. To hatch an egg the player must put it in a incubator then walk either 2k,5k or 10k. The amount of distance required to hatch an egg corresponds with the rarity of the pokemon.
